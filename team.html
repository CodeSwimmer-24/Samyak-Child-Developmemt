Manisha Gaurav

Pathologist

Manisha Gaurav is a seasoned Speech and Language Pathologist with 18 years of experience. An alumna of Ali Yavar Jung National Institute, she has worked across leading organizations such as Udaan for Disabilities, Sampoorn, Shritik Foundation, Needer Vaishali, Neurodiverse Srinagar, and VIMHANS, contributing significantly to neurodevelopmental and communication support services.

Vikas Sharma

Senior Occupational Therapist

Vikas Sharma is a Senior Occupational Therapist with over 12 years of experience in pediatric and developmental therapy. An alumnus of Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Institute, Delhi University, he is the founder of Shritik Foundation and Sampoorn Child Development Centre, both dedicated to supporting children with special needs through inclusive and holistic care.

Aditya Kamal

Physical Therapist

Aditya Kamal, also known as Adi Rehab, is a new-age Physical Therapist with over 5 years of clinical experience. A graduate of Amity University, Noida, he is the founder of The Physiometric (2019) and Body Talks™ (2021). Aditya specializes in Structural Integration, Posture Reset, and Movement Therapy, and is pioneering innovative assessment and treatment systems in India.
